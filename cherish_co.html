Motivation

The motivation behind enhancing Cherish Co.'s digital platform stems from a belief in the transformative power of education and care for vulnerable children. Supporting the organization's mission to provide shelter, learning opportunities, and emotional well-being for orphans inspired the design and development of a website and mobile app that foster awareness, engagement, and community support. The goal was to create a digital presence that effectively communicates the organization's impact while encouraging broader participation and contributions to its cause.
Final Design

The team worked on creating a cohesive brand identity, including the design of the organization's logo, while I specifically focused on crafting the "About" and "Contact" pages for both the website and Android app, ensuring a user-friendly and impactful experience.
