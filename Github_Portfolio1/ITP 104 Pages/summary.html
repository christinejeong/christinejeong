Summary

Topic and Purpose

As a graphic designer ever since middle school, I wanted to create a site where I can organize and overview all the work I've created over the years. This will be helpful when I want to view all my artwork at once and also submit it to others who may want to view my work. This portfolio showcases my artworks and introduces me both as a person and a designer, engineer, and an entrepreneur. The “work” section in the portfolio website is divided into four sub-pages of “graphic design,” “ui/ux,” “video,” and “photography,” each of which showcases artworks of its medium. The about page is a description of my background and pathways in digital media, and the contact page allows the users to enter their information and send me a message.

Extras

For the contact page, I created a form in which users can fill out the boxes with their information and write a message to submit it to me.

In the home page, I incorporated transitions into four boxes that are each labeled as one of the art mediums in my portfolio. As users hover their mouse over the boxes, each of the boxes increase and change its text and box colors in order.
